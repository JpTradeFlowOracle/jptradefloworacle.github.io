The Service

The Service provides structured, machine-readable economic indicators for the Japanese market — including CPI deviation deltas, FX-adjusted deltas, and the SJ-Score cost-justification model — derived from public government and institutional data sources (e-Stat, FRED, USDA GATS/MARS, Bank of Japan, Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare). Output is returned via the /analyze and /summary endpoints described in the Service's OpenAPI specification.

Access & Authentication

Access is granted per-request upon successful payment through one of the following channels:

Requests without a valid payment credential will receive an HTTP 402 Payment Required response and no data will be returned.
