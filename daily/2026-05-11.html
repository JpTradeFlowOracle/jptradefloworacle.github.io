What is EDINET and why is this data important?

EDINET (Electronic Disclosure for Investors NETwork) is the official disclosure platform operated by Japan's Financial Services Agency. It contains mandatory filings including tender offers, large shareholder reports (5%+ ownership changes), extraordinary reports, and share buyback disclosures.
