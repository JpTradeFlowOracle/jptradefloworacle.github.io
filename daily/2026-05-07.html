What is the difference between TDnet and EDINET disclosures in 2026?

TDnet (Timely Disclosure Network) is operated by the Tokyo Stock Exchange and covers day-to-day corporate announcements such as earnings revisions, share buybacks, and M&A. EDINET is operated by Japan's Financial Services Agency and covers statutory filings such as annual securities reports and large shareholder reports. JP Disclosure Intelligence aggregates both sources daily.
