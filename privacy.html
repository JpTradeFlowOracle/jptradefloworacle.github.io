JP Disclosure Intelligence ("this site," "we," "us") publishes English-language summaries of Japanese corporate disclosures sourced from EDINET (Financial Services Agency of Japan). This page explains what data is collected when you visit this site and how it is used.

Information We Collect

This site itself does not require account creation, and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name or email address through any form on this site.

However, like most websites, this site may automatically collect certain technical information through cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website (the page that linked you here)
  • Approximate location based on IP address

Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense and other third-party advertising networks. These networks may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your visits to this and other websites, in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. For more information on how Google uses data when you use partner sites, see Google's Partner Sites policy.

Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use this site in aggregate. These services may use cookies to collect anonymized usage data. No personally identifiable information is shared with us through these services.

Source Data

The disclosure data presented on this site is sourced from EDINET, operated by Japan's Financial Services Agency (FSA), and is translated and structured for English-language readability. This site does not collect or process any personal information contained within the original filings beyond what is already publicly disclosed by the filers themselves.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended for a general business and financial-data audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these settings in the "options" or "preferences" menu of your browser. You may also opt out of certain third-party advertising cookies through the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us via the project's GitHub repository.
