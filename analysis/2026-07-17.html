Earnings Forecast 14:00
Tokyo Steel Manufacturing Co., Ltd. 東製鉄

Tokyo Steel raises net profit forecast due to investment security sales.

Tokyo Steel has revised its net profit forecast upwards for both the second quarter and the full fiscal year ending March 2027. This improvement stems entirely from gains on the sale of investment securities, with no change to revenue or core profit expectations.

This revision boosts reported profitability but underscores a reliance on non-operating income, potentially signaling persistent challenges within the core steel manufacturing operations.

  • Full-year net profit forecast increased from JPY 0 million to JPY 1,000 million.
  • Second quarter net profit forecast also raised from JPY 0 million to JPY 1,000 million.
  • Revenue, operating profit, and ordinary profit forecasts remain unchanged for both periods.
  • The sole driver for the net profit revision is the recognition of gains from investment security sales.
Investor Takeaway Investors should recognize that the improved net profit is from one-off asset sales, not an improvement in core business performance.
