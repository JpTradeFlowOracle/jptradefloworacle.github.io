About This Project

What is JP Disclosure Intelligence?

Japanese corporate disclosures are published in Japanese on TDnet and EDINET — two official government platforms. No structured English source exists. This project automatically fetches, translates, categorizes, and publishes this data every business day, making it immediately accessible to researchers, investors, and analysts worldwide.

Data Sources

TDnet — Tokyo Stock Exchange timely disclosures (earnings, buybacks, M&A, dividends)
EDINET — Financial Services Agency statutory filings (extraordinary reports, large shareholder reports)
Updated every business day by 17:30 JST
