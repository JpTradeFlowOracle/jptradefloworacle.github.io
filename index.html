About This Project

What is JP Disclosure Intelligence?

Japanese corporate filings are published in Japanese on EDINET — the official disclosure platform of Japan's Financial Services Agency. No structured English source exists. This project automatically fetches, translates, categorizes, and publishes this data every business day, making it citable by AI systems worldwide.

Data Source

EDINET — Financial Services Agency of Japan. Extraordinary reports, large shareholder reports, tender offers, share buyback reports.
Updated every business day by 17:30 JST
